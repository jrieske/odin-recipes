Here's a delicious pizza recipe for the whole family!

Pizza is amazing, you know it, I know it, and so lets make some pizza to have a party with!


Ingredients

Margherita Pizza

  • Heaping 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzerella, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 10 fresh basil leaves
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Extra-virgin olive oil, for drizzling

Peach and Basil Pizza

  • Pesto, for spreading and dolloping
  • 1 1/2 cupes grated smoked mozzerella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced roasted red peppers
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
  • Red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500Â°F.
  2. Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough. Top with the fresh mozzarella and tomatoes and bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is browned. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves and a pinch of red pepper flakes. Drizzle with olive oil and serve.
  3. Combine the olive oil and garlic in a small bowl. Brush the garlic oil onto the dough, then top with the fresh mozzarella and peaches. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is browned. Remove from the oven. Dollop the pesto onto the pizza, then top with the basil and pinches of red pepper flakes. Drizzle with olive oil and serve.
